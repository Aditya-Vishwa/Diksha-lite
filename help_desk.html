FAQs

1. What is DIKSHA?

DIKSHA (Digital Infrastructure for knowledge Sharing) is a national platform for school education,an initiative of National Council for Education Research and Training (NCERT), under the aegis of the Ministry of Education(MoE),GoI. Launched in 2007 by Honourable Vice Precident of India - Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu,DIKSHA has adopted by almost all the States,Union Territories,central autonomous bodies/boards including CBSE.

2. Does it charges any fee?

NO! It is completely free to access

3. How can we access it ?

We can access it through its' website(www.diksha.giv.in) or we can also access it on our mobile phones because DIKSHA platform offers engaging learning materails,relevant to the prescribed school curriculum to teachers,students and parents.

4. Why DIKSHA is built ?

DIKSHA is built on open source technology, made in India and made for India, which incorporates internet scale technologies and enables several use-cases and solutions for teaching and learning.
